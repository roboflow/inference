# Community Plugins

Roboflow Workflows is built to be extended. Community plugins let you bring custom blocks, models, and 
integrations into the Workflows ecosystem — contributed by developers who saw a gap and filled it. Whether you need a 
specialized pre-processing step, a connector to a third-party service, or an entirely new inference pipeline, 
chances are someone in the community has already built it.

Browse the directory below to discover available plugins, or [learn how to build and publish your own](./blocks_bundling.md).


!!! warning "Third-party software"
    
    The plugins listed on this page are developed and maintained by their respective authors, not by 
    Roboflow. We feature them here to support the community and make discovery easier, but we do not 
    review, endorse, or guarantee their functionality, security, or compatibility. Please evaluate 
    each plugin on your own — review the source code, check licensing terms, and test thoroughly in 
    your environment before using it in production.


## `sportvision`

`sportvision` is a sports analytics toolkit that ships 4 Roboflow Workflow blocks for calculating distance between
players, team classification by jersey color and tracking ball posession over time.

PyPI: [https://pypi.org/project/sportvision/](https://pypi.org/project/sportvision/)
GitHub: [https://github.com/MohibShaikh/sportvision](https://github.com/MohibShaikh/sportvision)
